Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our jewellery, services, and processes

What types of gold do you work with?
We work with various gold purities including 18K, 21K, and 22K gold. Each has its own characteristics in terms of color, durability, and value. Our experts can help you choose the right gold type for your specific needs and preferences.
How long does custom design take?
Custom design timelines vary depending on the complexity of the piece. Typically, the process takes 3-6 weeks from initial consultation to final delivery. This includes design consultation, CAD rendering, approval, crafting, and final finishing. Rush orders may be accommodated for an additional fee.
Do you offer warranties on your jewellery?
Yes! All our jewellery comes with a comprehensive lifetime warranty covering manufacturing defects and craftsmanship issues. We also offer complimentary cleaning and inspection services for the life of your piece. Extended warranties for accidental damage are available for purchase.
Can you resize rings?
Absolutely! We offer professional ring resizing services for most ring styles. The process typically takes 1-2 weeks depending on the complexity. Some rings with intricate designs or certain gemstone settings may have limitations, which our experts will discuss with you during consultation.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods including cash, credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and bank transfers. For custom orders, we typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the balance due upon completion. Payment plans may be available for larger purchases.
Do you buy or trade old gold jewellery?
Yes, we do! We offer competitive rates for purchasing old gold jewellery and also accept trade-ins toward new purchases. The value is determined by the current gold market price and the purity of your gold. Bring your pieces in for a free, no-obligation evaluation.
